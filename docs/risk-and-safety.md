# Risk And Safety

## Hardware risk policy

Before flashing or wiring devices, document:

- power input method and voltage,
- logic voltage compatibility,
- boot/strapping pin interactions,
- relay coil/input current requirements,
- radio antenna and regulatory constraints,
- isolation requirements for relay loads,
- recovery process for failed flashing.

## Current safety status

The scaffold does not authorize live wiring, live flashing, or relay load
switching. Those actions require a task-specific bench plan.
